About us

What is Sebastian?

Sebastian is a productivity tool meant to make your life easier on campus. Think of him as your own personal butler at Harvard. He aggregates all of the services provided by various organizations in the area into one place so that you have a streamlined experience of getting stuff done.

When you here with Sebastian, you get access to additional features, such as automated information input and scheduling functions so that your transactions can be remembered and repeated. Plus, get a variety of discounts and special deals from our partnering services for being a loyal customer!

How does Sebastian partner up with services?

We know you're busy and the things you need done are important. Sebastian is committed to finding partner organizations who are good at getting stuff done, and masters at being reliable, affordable, and student friendly. Check out our partner organizations here, or suggest a new one we should get in touch with here!

What else can Sebastian do for me?

Theoretically, anything and everything. Sebastian is a changing, living, learning service that strives to not only fulfill needs around campus, but also find out what they are. You can help us do that by suggesting a service for Sebastian, or send us general comments here!
