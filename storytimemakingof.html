The Making of a Story

This project started as a collaborative effort between two sisters, for the sake of learning/teaching how to write creatively, and to see what can happen when you put the ever active mind of an 8 year old to the test. Below is the journey that they embarked on, hope you all enjoy.

* The creative team at Sebastian is not entirely well versed in the art of the English language, nor are they actually certified to teach creative writing. If any editors/geniuses at large/English concentrators/grammar police out there would like to contribute to this project as an advising writer, please sent a query to heysebastianandco@gmail.com or click here. Thanks!
